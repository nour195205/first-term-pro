Mark Zuckerberg


He is an American businessman and programmer, born in White Plains, New York, USA, on May 14, 1984.


Mark Zuckerberg's upbringing


Mark Zuckerberg grew up in a wealthy Jewish family. His father was a doctor, and his mother was a dentist. Mark was a very smart child and loved math and science. At the age of 12, he learned computer programming.

Founding of Facebook


In 2003, Mark Zuckerberg was a sophomore at Harvard University. He created a website called "TheFacebook" to help students communicate with each other. The site quickly became popular, and in 2004, it changed its name to “Facebook”.


Mark Zuckerberg's contribution to society

Mark Zuckerberg contributed $50 million to create the Chan Zuckerberg Initiative, a non-profit organization focused on education, health, and international development.v


Mark Zuckerberg's influence on the world

Mark Zuckerberg has had a huge impact on the world. It changed the way people communicate with each other and helped spread information and culture around the world.
